COFFEE TABLE

A coffee table is a low and usually long table that is placed in the living room in front of a sofa or seating area.
It's called a "coffee table" because it's often used to hold items like coffee cups, drinks, books, magazines, and decorative objects

FUNCTIONS OF COFFEE TABLE

  • It's a good spot for the TV remote, tablets, or laptops
  • It's good for holding snacks and drinks while you watch TV
  • People can sit around it, chat, and play games together
